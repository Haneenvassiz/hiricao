Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last updated: January 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Hirica, an AI-powered hiring platform operated by TetraQuad ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, whether as a job seeker or recruiter. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal information.

Key Point: By using Hirica, you consent to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, phone number
  • Profile Information: Professional experience, education, skills, resume
  • Company Information: Company name, position, LinkedIn profile (for recruiters)
  • Application Data: Cover letters, assessment responses, project submissions
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, support inquiries

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use our platform, we automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns, search queries
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address
  • Cookies: Session data, preferences, authentication tokens

2.3 Assessment and Anti-Cheat Data

During assessments, we collect:

  • Video/Audio Data: Webcam recordings and audio monitoring for proctoring
  • Behavioral Data: Keystroke patterns, mouse movements, tab switching
  • System Data: Screen resolution, browser focus, application switching
  • Response Data: Assessment answers, time taken, completion status
Important: Proctoring data is collected only during active assessments and is used solely for maintaining assessment integrity.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

3.1 Platform Operations

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Facilitating job applications and recruitment processes
  • Generating AI-powered assessments and evaluations
  • Matching job seekers with relevant opportunities
  • Processing payments and transactions

3.2 Communication

  • Sending assessment invitations and results
  • Notifying about application status updates
  • Providing customer support and technical assistance
  • Sending platform updates and important announcements

3.3 Platform Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Developing new features and services
  • Training AI models for better assessment generation
  • Conducting research and analytics

3.4 Security and Compliance

  • Maintaining assessment integrity through anti-cheat measures
  • Preventing fraud and unauthorized access
  • Complying with legal obligations and regulations
  • Protecting the rights and safety of our users

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Recruiters and Employers

When you apply for a job, we share your application information, resume, assessment results, and other relevant data with the respective recruiter or employer.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Cloud hosting and data storage
  • Email delivery and communication services
  • AI and machine learning services (Groq API)
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Payment processing

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes, subpoenas, or court orders
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigate potential violations of our Terms of Service
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activities

4.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity as part of the business transaction.

We Never: Sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes or share assessment results with unauthorized parties.

5. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

5.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication and role-based access restrictions
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection, and secure hosting infrastructure
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and system updates applied promptly

5.2 Organizational Safeguards

  • Employee training on data protection and privacy policies
  • Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Incident response procedures for security breaches

5.3 Assessment Security

  • Secure proctoring systems with encrypted video/audio transmission
  • Real-time monitoring for suspicious activity
  • Tamper-resistant assessment delivery mechanisms
  • Secure storage of assessment responses and results
Important: While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage users to use strong passwords and keep login credentials confidential.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience:

6.1 Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze usage and improve our services
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Provide insights into user behavior and platform performance

6.2 Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics services such as:

  • Google Analytics for website traffic analysis
  • Performance monitoring tools
  • Social media integration services

6.3 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may impact platform functionality. Our cookie banner allows you to manage preferences for non-essential cookies.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Obtain a copy of your data in a portable format
  • View your assessment results and application history

7.2 Correction and Update

  • Update your profile information at any time
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Modify your communication preferences

7.3 Deletion and Restriction

  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Restrict processing of your information in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing

7.4 Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Manage notification settings
  • Control frequency of platform updates
Exercising Your Rights: Contact us at privacy@hirica.com to exercise any of these rights.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for the following periods:

8.1 Account Data

  • Active Accounts: Retained while your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Deleted after 3 years of inactivity
  • Closed Accounts: Most data deleted within 30 days of closure

8.2 Assessment Data

  • Assessment Results: Retained for 7 years for verification purposes
  • Proctoring Videos: Deleted after 90 days unless flagged for violations
  • Anti-Cheat Logs: Retained for 2 years for security analysis

8.3 Legal and Compliance

  • Information retained as required by applicable laws
  • Data related to legal proceedings preserved until resolution
  • Security incident logs retained for investigation purposes

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by relevant authorities
  • Adequacy decisions for transfers to countries with adequate protection
  • Appropriate technical and organizational security measures
  • Regular reviews of transfer mechanisms and protections

10. Children's Privacy

Hirica is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

If you believe we have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately at privacy@hirica.com.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our platform
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Displaying prominent notices on our website
  • Updating the "Last Modified" date at the top of this policy

Your continued use of Hirica after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Email: privacy@hirica.com
